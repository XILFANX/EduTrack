# Getting Started with EduTrack

Welcome to EduTrack! EduTrack is a unified school management platform that connects Principals, Teachers, Bursars, and Parents into a single digital ecosystem.

## Logging In

If you have been invited to EduTrack by your school, you do not need to create an account from scratch. 

1. Check your email or WhatsApp for a secure invitation link from your School Principal or Class Teacher.
2. Click the link on your computer or mobile device.
3. You will be prompted to set a secure password.
4. Once set, you will be instantly redirected to your personalized dashboard!

[SCREENSHOT: /dashboard — The EduTrack welcome dashboard immediately after first login]

> Tip: If you lose your invitation link, or it expires, please contact your school administrator to generate a new one.

## Navigating the Portal

Once logged in, the interface you see depends on your role at the school:

- **Principals** will see the grand overview of school operations, staff, and finances.
- **Teachers** will see their assigned classes, attendance registers, and exam entry screens.
- **Bursars** will see fee structures, invoicing tools, and payment ledgers.
- **Parents** will see an aggregated view of their children's report cards, attendance logs, and fee balances.
