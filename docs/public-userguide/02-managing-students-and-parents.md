# Managing Students & Parents

This guide explains how to add students to EduTrack and link them to their parents.

## Adding Students
Principals and administrative staff can admit new students into the system.

1. Go to the **Students** tab on the main dashboard.
2. Click **Admit Student**.
3. Enter the student's legal name, Date of Birth, and unique **Admission Number**.
4. Select the class they are being admitted into.
5. Click **Save Student**.

[SCREENSHOT: /dashboard/students/new — The student admission form]

> Note: The Admission Number must be unique across the entire school. The system will reject duplicates to prevent errors.

[Link: /dashboard/students]

## Inviting Parents
Class Teachers are responsible for inviting the parents of students in their assigned class.

1. From the **Teacher Portal**, select your assigned class.
2. View the student roster and click **Invite Parent** next to a student's name.
3. A secure token link will be generated specifically linked to that child.
4. Send the link to the parent.
5. When the parent clicks the link, they create their account and are permanently linked to that student's record.

[SCREENSHOT: /teacher/students — The class roster showing the 'Invite Parent' button next to each student]

### What happens if a parent has multiple children in the school?
If a parent already has an EduTrack account for their first child, they simply need to click the invite link for their second child, log in, and the system will automatically merge the siblings under a single parent dashboard.
